Support Coordination

Support Coordination is a service designed to assist individuals with an NDIS (National Disability Insurance Scheme) plan in getting the most out of their available supports. Essentially, a Support Coordinator serves as a personal guide, helping participants understand their plans, choose the right service providers, and connect with valuable resources and services within their community. Their role is to simplify the complexities of the NDIS and empower participants to access the best supports tailored to their needs..

Am I eligible for Support Coordination?

To access Support Coordination, you'll need to check if it has been included in your NDIS plan. This service is funded through the Capacity Building Supports budget, specifically under the category of 'Support Coordination'. If it's part of your plan, you'll be able to work with a Support Coordinator to help you navigate and maximise the benefits of your NDIS supports.

How Can a Bluegum Support Coordinator Help You?

Your Bluegum Ability Support Coordinator can assist you in various ways, including:

  • Helping you to build your NDIS plan so it meets your goals and needs.
  • Helping you understand and make the most of your NDIS plan.
  • Connecting you with the right providers for the supports you’re funded for.
  • Supporting you in achieving your personal goals.
  • Accessing essential healthcare and education services.
  • Increasing your participation in the community.
  • Assisting with job search efforts.
  • Addressing your accommodation needs.
  • Building your confidence to independently manage your supports.
