What is Supported Independent Living or SIL

Supported Independent Living (SIL) is a type of support that helps individuals with disabilities live independently in shared or individual accommodations. It provides assistance with daily tasks and can be included as part of your NDIS plan to help you achieve greater independence and improve your quality of life.
